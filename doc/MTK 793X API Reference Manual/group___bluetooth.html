Overview

MediaTek LinkIt development platform for RTOS provides Bluetooth Low Energy (LE) connectivity support for IoT and Wearable's applications.

Devices that support Bluetooth Low Energy are referred to as single-mode devices where the low power consumption is the primary concern for application development, such as those that run on coin cell batteries.
